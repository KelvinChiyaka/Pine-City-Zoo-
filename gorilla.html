GORILLA

The gorilla (Gorilla gorilla) is the largest living primate and is native to the forests of central and western Africa. Known for their impressive size and strength, gorillas are primarily herbivorous, feeding on leaves, stems, and fruit. They live in social groups led by a dominant silverback.

Gorillas are highly intelligent animals, exhibiting complex social behaviors and communication skills. They are known for their gentle nature, despite their formidable appearance. Gorillas play a crucial role in their ecosystem by helping to maintain the health of the forests they inhabit through seed dispersal.
